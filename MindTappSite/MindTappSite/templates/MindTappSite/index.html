About

We Are MindTAPP

  • Who We Are

    MindTAPP is a human resources and mindset development group. Our unique solution blends consulting, training, and smartphone gaming technology to help our clients train, change, and develop psychological belief systems that are strategically aligned to improve performance. Our research-based solutions complement and support your existing programs and organizational goals to TAPP the best potential in your employees and leaders.

  • What we do

    How successful are your company initiatives? U.S. companies spend $70 billion every year on employee training and development, with the vast majority (75%) ending in failure. These programs primarily focus on behavioral change, with little consideration of the corresponding need to change and align mindset. MindTAPP’s unique learning solution cultivates the psychological belief systems necessary to bring about behavioral change through fun smartphone based brain games involving puzzles, memory tasks, and mental challenges. Our approach is supported by science and empirical evidence demonstrating that mindset training is the most effective learning solution.

  • Why MindTAPP?

    Our creativity and innovation sets us apart. MindTAPP stands alone in offering services and proprietary technology for mindset alignment with organizational goals. We partner with you to understand your biggest challenges and develop custom learning solutions aimed at achieving your goals. We are both scientists and learning solution architects, bringing research-based rigor, along with our insights and innovation to develop solutions unique to your organization. Together, we empower you to TAPP your best self and unleash your potential.

Thomas Sy

Dr. Thomas Sy is an award-winning scholar, teacher and consultant. He is on the faculty of the Department of Psychology at the University of California where he teaches and conducts research on leadership, teams, unconscious biases, and diversity. He is Founder and CEO of MindTAPP, a tech startup developing solutions for cultivating inclusive minds and leadership. Dr. Sy completed his PhD in Organizational Psychology from the University of Michigan. He was formerly an advisor with a top-tier global management consultancy, specializing in corporate strategy and operations. Dr. Sy has also served in the U.S. Army Special Forces (Green Beret).

Dr. Sy provides consulting, training, and coaching services to industry. He has served a variety of client organizations, including General Motors, Google, Zappos, Los Angeles County, Northrop Grumman, Salvation Army, EDS, Ford, Southern California Edison, Waste Management, Johnson & Johnson, Boeing, IBM, General Electric, U.S. Army Research Institute, U.S. Army Corps of Engineers, and the United States Marine Corp Special Operations Command.

His research has been published in a variety of outlets, including the Journal of Applied Psychology, Organizational Behavior and Human Decision Processes, Leadership Quarterly, Journal of Organizational Behavior, Journal of Vocational Behavior, Asian American Journal of Psychology, as well appearing in popular media such as National Public Radio, London Times, Washington Post, and among others.
